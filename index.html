Welcome to Kahũa Coffee

Nestled in the lush green hills bordering Muranga and Kiambu counties of Kenya's Central Province, Kahũa Coffee thrives on 35 acres of rich volcanic soil and an ideal climate. Our coffee farm produces some of the finest single-origin Arabica beans, celebrated for their distinctive flavor profiles. At Kahũa Coffee, we believe in a harmonious relationship with nature. We employ sustainable practices that minimize chemical use while nurturing the environment and preserving the integrity of our beans.

Our story is one of passion and purpose

Kahũa Coffee is proudly owned by a visionary woman entrepreneur dedicated to crafting worldclass coffee while uplifting her community. As a small business, we cherish the personal connections we forge along the way, ensuring that every step, from planting to harvesting, to processing, is done with care and precision. From Bean to Cup Our commitment to quality reflects our deep-rooted belief in the superiority of Kenyan coffee and the passion of the people who make it possible. At Kahũa Coffee, we pride ourselves on owning the entire process—from bean to cup. By overseeing every stage of production, we guarantee the consistency and excellence that defines our brand. Every sip of our coffee tells a story: the richness of fertile lands, the dedication of our hands-on approach, and the vibrant heritage of Kenyan coffee. Whether shared among friends or enjoyed in quiet moments, Kahũa Coffee is more than just a beverage; It's a celebration of craftsmanship, community, and connection.

"At Kahũa, we aspire to be more than just a coffee brand. We envision becoming a global ambassador for Kenyan coffee, bringing the taste of Thika's hills to coffee enthusiasts around the world. Through our commitment to quality, tradition, and sustainability, we aim to redefine the premium coffee experience."

Vision

Cultivation: Thika Kenya

Kahũa's journey begins in the fertile volcanic soil of Thika, Kenya. Coffee plants thrive in this unique environment, absorbing the rich minerals from the earth, contributing to the distinct flavor profile of Kahũa coffee.

Meticulous Selection of Cherries

Kahũa cherries are delicately hand-harvested, emphasizing the commitment to quality. This manual approach allows our farmers to select only the finest cherries, ensuring a consistent and exceptional coffee bean.

Enhancing Flavour Details

Our roasting process is an art form, focusing on enhancing the nuanced flavors present in the beans. Whether it's the boldness of the dark roast or the subtleties of a medium roast, Kahũa coffee is crafted to deliver a unique and memorable taste experience.

Packaging for Freshness

Once the beans have undergone the perfect roast, they are promptly sealed in specially designed packaging to preserve freshness. This final step ensures that when you open a bag of Kahũa coffee, you are greeted with the same rich aroma and bold flavor experienced during the roasting process.
